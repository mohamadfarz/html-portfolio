The Best Movies According to Farz

My top 3 movies of all-time

Burnt

This is my favourite movie. It shows the love and passion for the work and how to achieve things you want the most.

Sound of Metal

This is more of a philosophical movie that teaches you how to accept the loss and make peace with the reality and present condition.

A Seperation

It's a beautiful movie about the seperation of a husband and wife. It also shows how the kids suffer from the fight and ego of their parents.
